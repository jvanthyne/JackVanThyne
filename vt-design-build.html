VT DESIGN BUILD
We are a design-build firm specializing in smaller structures — from saunas to art installations to custom furniture. Occasionally, we take on preliminary design work for larger projects, but our focus is on smaller-scale work because we are also full-time students.
OUR PHILOSOPHY
We specialize in wood structures and enjoy being involved in every stage of the process — from initial idea to finished reality. Each project is approached thoughtfully, balancing functionality, sustainability, and craft.
FEATURED PROJECTS
Our work includes Floating Sauna and Mobile Sauna, the Circle Stage, and Time Machine, among many others. To see more of our projects, take a look at the Archives.

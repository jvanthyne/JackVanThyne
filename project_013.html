GRID LIGHT- 2024

This is my third lighting project — it represents one of my first successful implementations of highly precise joinery work. — I spent about a week just getting the grid joints accurate, followed by another two to three weeks soldering lighting, building a housing, and creating a diffused pane of sandblasted acrylic. — This project was completely made of scrap wood, similar to many of my projects. — The grid was made from mahogany strips from a bed frame, while the housing and structure were made from a cherry headboard.

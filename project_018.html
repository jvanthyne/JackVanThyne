OUTDOOR TABLES

2023 – I love cooking, and this project is somewhat related — I built tables for an outdoor dinner party on the beach for my mom's birthday. I used very thin scrap wood to create the lattice pattern of the tables, and the legs could be removed for easy transport to the site and assembly. I prepared the entire dinner over a fire on the beach with the custom wood tables for my mom and her friends. I had visions of starting a supper club and took inspiration from OutstandingintheField.

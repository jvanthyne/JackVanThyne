3716 FORTUNATO WAY- 2021

This project occurred during COVID — I was spending a lot of time at home, especially outdoors. — Inspired to renovate and upgrade the yard at my home, I offered to design and execute a full yard makeover. — This was my first experience working for a client, in a sense, although I had a bit more freedom. — I wasn't without constraints, though. — I spent all my time working on the yard, often having my computer running Zoom classes on the porch while I graded and tended the soil, removing old plants. — I designed and built a composite deck, created custom low-voltage landscape lighting, developed an irrigation scheme, and chose plants — all while doing the manual labor myself. — I truly spent every day working on some aspect of the project. — All in all, the project took about three months to complete.

HIGH SCHOOL ARCHITECTURE COMPETITION- 2021

This project began two years before I entered the competition, starting during my freshman year of high school. — Encouraged by a teacher’s recommendation, I signed up for an architecture competition. — The competition involved a full day of design, where I was tasked with creating hand-drawn sections, plans, elevations, and details — concepts I had never encountered before. — Although I faced significant challenges, the experience ignited my passion for architecture. — I spent the next year practicing my drawing skills and learning techniques through online resources. — When I returned to the competition the following year, I won, competing against approximately 100 entries. — This pivotal moment motivated me to pursue a life of design.

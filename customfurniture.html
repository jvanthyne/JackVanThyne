about – van thyne custom furniture

i’m jack van thyne, a student at uc berkeley studying landscape architecture with a focus on furniture design and research in wood construction and forest management.

i design and build custom furniture in the bay area, often using reclaimed or locally sourced lumber. each project is a small collaboration between you, the site, and the material.

my work sits between architecture, furniture, and landscape. i like pieces that feel honest about their structure: you can read how they’re built and where the wood came from.

materials are treated as a record of place and use. dents, grain shifts, and repairs are part of the design language, not something to hide.

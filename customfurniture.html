Welcome

Welcome to Van Thyne Custom Furniture. I am a student at UC Berkeley studying landscape architecture with a focus on furniture design and research in wood construction and forest management.

I design and build custom furniture in the Bay Area, working closely with clients to create pieces that feel rooted, durable, and personal.

Design Philosophy.txt

My work lives somewhere between architecture, furniture, and landscape. I like pieces that feel honest about their structure: you can read how they’re built and where the wood came from.

Whenever possible, I use reclaimed or locally sourced material and treat every project as a collaboration between client, site, and wood. The goal is furniture that can be lived with, repaired, and passed on.

Every project starts with a conversation about how you actually use a space: sitting, eating, working, gathering. From there we sketch, model, and iterate until the form feels inevitable.

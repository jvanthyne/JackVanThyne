INFO: Lighting Design/Build Competition

All entries must be designed to hang from a wire or cable. There are no restrictions on size.

Participants may either purchase a bulb and socket set with wire for $10 by emailing bluetilespace@gmail.com, or source their own components from scrap or discarded fixtures.

The competition is intentionally open-ended. For inspiration, consider themes of biophilic design and the moon.

Prizes will be awarded to the top three most visually compelling designs.

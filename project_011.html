PAINTINGS- 2022

I have always liked to draw and paint — I really began to get into painting over the summer between my sophomore and junior years. — I focused on landscape paintings, using primarily wood canvas with acrylic, mainly because that was what was available. — I found scrap wood, sanded and cut it to size, and I also have a massive collection of paint that I acquired from a friend who was an artist and passed away. — My roommates share this passion for art, and we converted our garage into a painting studio. — Here are some of my favorite pieces. — I also used scrap wood from the fabrication shop to build custom frames for all of my pieces.

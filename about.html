ABOUT ME- 2004-NOW

Introduction: I’m Jack Van Thyne, a 21-year-old design student, artist and builder. I attend UC Berkeley studying landscape architecture and sustainable design, and am currently in Copenhagen Denmark studying designing and building furniture. Medium and Materials: My favorite medium is wood, as it offers a very natural tactile interaction that allows a deep connection with the material, you can feel and interact with the form like no other material, you can tell where it is from, there is so primal/human about this natural material. I also like working with metal, light, watercolor, film, and music each serving a unique purpose in my creative process. Artistic Philosophy: The goal of my work is to curate feeling through design, creating art, space and objects that facilitates new ways of thinking and alters how we experience the world around us. By bringing fresh perspectives to the familiar, my work aims to create moments of emotional resonance and deepen our engagement with the world we live in. I love designing projects that are designed and built to last, and yet at the same time also designed with an afterlife in mind, think about what this object or space Sustainability:Sustainability is rooted at the core of my design process. It’s about building a thoughtful connection to the world around us, ensuring sustainable design is woven into every step of the process and reflected in the choices we make.

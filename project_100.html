DOVETAIL CHAIR

2023 – During a semester in Denmark studying furniture design, I built this project using a single sheet of plywood. The piece was designed to pack flat, used no glue, and featured hand-cut dovetail joints.

The process of creating the chair emphasized precision, patience, and respect for material. Every cut and fold was deliberate, reflecting the interplay between function, form, and the unique properties of the plywood. The resulting piece not only folds flat for transport but also maintains structural integrity and visual elegance.

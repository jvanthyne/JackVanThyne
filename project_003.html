WESTMONT CONCERT HALL- 2022

This project is the result of my summer internship at AB Design Studio in Santa Barbara. I spent the majority of my time on a chapel project sited at Westmont School, which was a conceptual design for a new chapel/concert hall. Most of my time was dedicated to researching building techniques and working in CAD to create a digital file of my proposed design intervention. I became very familiar with CAD and also experimented with rendering software. The design featured a concrete structure with steel and glass curtain walls, made using Grasshopper, along with an exoskeleton constructed from composite panels.
